All in

released on May 17th 2016

MV commentary by the members

There are many theories behind this music video, one could even freely find their own meanings in everything it has to offer to the viewers. However, from everything I have read online that goes from lyrics of the song to public comments wrote by fans, I'll try to sum up quick what's most important in my humble opinion.

I am very attached to this music video as it was released when Monsta x were only one year into their career and the whole storyline added to their acting can deeply move whose who are sensitive to themes such as homosexuality, poverty and in general degrading realities people go through for simply wanting to express their strong desire to obtain freedom.

From the beginning the song sounds impactful, almost disquieting. Minhyuk giving an unconscious and collapsed Hyungwon a blue flower to eat in order for him to continue living symbolize they're not willing to surrender. The blue flower appears multiple times throughout the whole mv, it's called larkspur and its purpose is to heal. The two have obvious deep affection for each other which even leads Minhyuk to get revenge on the man who hurt Hyungwon (the boy tries to hide his bruised face with a mask from his lover), setting on fire the building that man supposedly lives in.

The music video continues showing an endearing reunion in which all the boys seem to be having fun being themselves and creating the healing potion that is probably their only medicine in that cruel world. Afterwards, Kihyun crawling and having obvious difficulties walking might symbolize the irreparable damage that fighting for freedom brought on his own body and which he needs to live with. Once again, it's clear why there's such an important focus on the larkspur flower.

Lastly, in the final part of the music video we can see all the boys looking at this strange bulb floating in the air that I assume might be the source of an unknown reality our protagonists want to touch and learn more about, leading to conclude the world they're currently living in is hopeless and they wouldn't mind abandoning any moment, of course continuing to stay together.
